About Cherie's

Cherish's Story

Founded in 2023 by Cherish Overseer, Cherie's Footwears was born from a lifelong passion for exceptional footwear and an unwavering commitment to quality.

With over 2 years of experience in luxury retail and a deep understanding of footwear craftsmanship, Cherish created a boutique that bridges the gap between high fashion and everyday comfort.

Each piece in our collection is personally curated by Cherish, ensuring that our clients receive only the finest footwear made from premium materials and impeccable construction.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to our most commonly asked questions. If you don't see what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

No, we do not offer international shipping at this time. We currently only ship within Nigeria.

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn shoes in their original packaging. Returns must be initiated within 30 days of delivery. For hygiene reasons, we cannot accept returns on items that show signs of wear or have been worn outside.

We recommend measuring your feet at the end of the day when they are at their largest. Stand on a piece of paper and trace the outline of your foot, then measure the length from heel to toe. Compare this measurement with our size chart available. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up.
